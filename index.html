About us

Weather Match is a unique match-3 puzzle game where players combine weather elements to solve challenges and complete tasks. With vibrant graphics, dynamic gameplay, and engaging challenges, the game is suitable for all ages and guarantees endless fun.

How to Play

  • Swipe to Match

    Connect three or more identical weather symbols.

  • Complete Objectives

    Fulfill the level's goals to advance.

  • Use Power-ups

    Activate powerful boosters to tackle difficult levels.

  • Plan Your Moves

    Strategize to make the most out of limited turns.

  • Compete with Friends

    Compare scores and progress with your friends.

FAQ

  • How do I start playing Weather Match?

    Download the app from the Play Store, launch the game, and complete the tutorial.

  • Is the game free?

    Yes, with optional in-app purchases for extra boosters and rewards.

  • Can I play offline?

    Absolutely! Weather Match supports offline gameplay.

  • What are the boosters, and how do I use them?

    Boosters are powerful tools that help you clear challenging levels. Activate them during gameplay.

  • Are there new levels added regularly?

    Yes, updates introduce fresh levels, features, and seasonal events.
